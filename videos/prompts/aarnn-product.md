# AARNN Product Video Prompt

Create a commercial product-video draft for AARNN.

Output sections in this order:
1. Video angle in one sentence
2. 90-135 second voiceover script
3. Shot-by-shot storyboard with on-screen text
4. Capture list tied to real product or demonstration surfaces
5. CTA slide copy
6. Facts to verify before recording

Ground the draft in these themes:
- AARNN is a neuroscience-led intelligence platform.
- Explain timed signal flow, morphology-aware execution, replay, and control in plain but precise language.
- Mention operator-style Model, Experiment, and Dataset surfaces.
- Connect the runtime story to embodiment through Webots.
- Keep the tone technical and credible rather than speculative.

Avoid:
- vague neuroscience metaphor without implementation detail
- generic AGI or consciousness language
