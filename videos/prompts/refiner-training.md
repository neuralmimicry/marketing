# Refiner Training Video Prompt

Create an operator-training draft for Refiner.

Output sections in this order:
1. Training objective
2. Audience and prerequisites
3. 5-8 minute walkthrough script
4. Step-by-step on-screen sequence
5. Operator checks after each major step
6. Terminology glossary for captions and transcript review
7. Risks or unknowns to verify before recording

Ground the walkthrough in this sequence:
- explain how to choose the right workflow
- show how context and prompts are supplied
- show where output artefacts appear and what good review looks like
- show where governance and operational controls live
- end with the limits of automation and where a human must still decide

Avoid:
- treating the workflow as pure chat
- skipping verification or approval points
