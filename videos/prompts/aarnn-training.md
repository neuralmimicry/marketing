# AARNN Training Video Prompt

Create an operator-training draft for AARNN.

Output sections in this order:
1. Training objective
2. Audience and prerequisites
3. 7-9 minute walkthrough script
4. Step-by-step on-screen sequence
5. Operator checks after each major step
6. Terminology glossary for captions and transcript review
7. Risks or unknowns to verify before recording

Ground the walkthrough in this sequence:
- establish the operator vocabulary for models, experiments, datasets, and runtime
- show what can be inspected and controlled
- explain how deployment and recruitment expand the runtime footprint
- connect the operator surface to embodiment and demonstrations
- close on what should still be verified manually

Avoid:
- purely theoretical narration
- claims that the runtime should be trusted without inspection
